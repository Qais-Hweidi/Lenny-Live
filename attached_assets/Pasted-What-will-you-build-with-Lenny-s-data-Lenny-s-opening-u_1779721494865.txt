How it works
1. Get the data
Clone the free starter pack from GitHub — 10 newsletter posts and 50 podcast transcripts in AI-friendly markdown. Paid subscribers can access the full archive (349 posts + 293 transcripts) at lennysdata.com.

2. Build on Replit
Sign in with your Replit account and start building. Use Replit Agent to go from idea to working app fast — no coding experience required. Think search tools, quizzes, games, AI assistants, visualizations, or anything else the data inspires.

3. Submit by May 27
Deploy your app on Replit and submit it through the portal. Include a demo link, screenshots, and a brief description of what you built and how it uses the dataset.

FAQ
Do I need to be a paid subscriber?
No! The free starter pack includes 10 newsletter posts and 50 podcast transcripts — plenty to build with. Paid subscribers get access to the full archive at lennysdata.com, but it's not required to participate.

When does it start and end?
The buildathon opens on May 6, 2026 and submissions close on May 27, 2026 at end of day. Winners will be announced on June 3, 2026.

Do I need to know how to code?
Not at all. LennyRPG was built by a non-technical designer using AI tools. Replit Agent lets you describe what you want to build in plain English and it writes the code for you.

What kind of things can I build?
Anything that uses Lenny's newsletter or podcast data in an interesting way — a search tool, a quiz, an AI assistant, a game, a visualization, a recommendation engine, a study guide. Check out example projects for inspiration.

Does my project need to be built on Replit?
Yes — your project must be built and deployed on Replit. You'll submit a .replit.app URL through the portal.

Who judges the winners?
The Replit team will review all submissions and announce winners on June 3, 2026. Projects are judged on creative use of the dataset, usefulness, and overall execution.
